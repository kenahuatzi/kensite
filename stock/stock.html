Asian American & Pacific Islander Heritage Month

May is Asian American and Pacific Islander (AAPI) Heritage Month, which celebrates the contributions and achievements of AAPI individuals throughout history. It was first established in 1978 as Asian Pacific American Heritage Week to commemorate the arrival of the first Japanese immigrants to the United States, but was expanded to a month-long celebration in 1992 in recognition of the many contributions of people of Asian and/or Pacific Islander-descent.
